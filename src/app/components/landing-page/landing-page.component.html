About

Find Out More About Us

The Andhra Pradesh state government embarked on this project by signing a memorandum of understanding (MoU) with the Gujarat Cooperative Milk Marketing Federation on 21 July 2020.

The project envisages strengthening of the dairy sector, besides conomic empowerment of women from rural areas by providing them a recurring income resource and a sustainable livelihood.

After milk pouring within 10 days of milk collection, the amount would be directly credited into the farmers’ accounts.

Features

Our Features

Hassle Free Payments

Money directly credited into farmer account with in 10 days payment cycle.

SMS Alerts

Farmer will be notified through sms whenever the payment credited into account.

Mobile app

Mobile apps are available in the play store to provide Transparency in the milk Procurement to the farmers.

Easy Enquiry

Farmer can directly contact volunteer for any queries.
